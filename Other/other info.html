Hospitals

Major health care service providers in Bloomington

There are many hospitals and individual health care service providers in and around Bloomington. Indiana University Health is one of the major health systems in B-town. Its partnership with Indiana University school of medicine provides a variety of advanced medical treatments and health related services.
