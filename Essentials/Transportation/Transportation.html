Transportation

Making your way through campus

From airport to campus

There are two shuttle options for getting from the airport to the campus:

  • GO Express Travel (website)
  • Star of America (website)

Both shuttles have almost similar fares and are the easiest way to commute to the campus from the airport if you are landing in the U.S. for the first time. Please check their websites since they have different drop-off locations.



From Bloomington to rest of the U.S.

You can choose from the following options to travel outside of Bloomington:

  • Air Travel You can book flight tickets through websites like Expedia, Kayak, Travelocity, or Priceline. Keep in mind that the nearest airport from Bloomington is the Indianapolis airport.
  • Bus To travel to neighboring cities via a bus there are some options like Catch a Ride (Student only), Hoosierride, or Greyhound.


Traveling on the campus

The popular options of travelling on campus are:

  • Bicycles You will find many faculty and students using bicycles to travel around campus. Bloomington is a bike frindly community and there are many shops to buy bicycles. You can buy a brand new bicycle or ge a used one from classifieds. (IDS classifieds).

  • Bus There are two bus services available for travelling on campus. These buses are the easiest and cheapest way to travel around the campus. Bloomington Transit has a wide coverage accross the campus and city. The other option is the Campus Bus service which covers only the campus. It is free to travel in these buses with your student ID card. Check further details here for Campus Bus, and here for Bloomington Transit.
