Restaurants

Some of the famous restaurants in Bloomington

Bub's Burgers & Ice Cream

The perfect hamburger joint! Get in here for some nice burgers, grub and old-fashioned malts. Check out more details here.



Samira Restaurant

This is a famous afghani restaurant in Bloomington. Its specialities include kebabs, dumplings and wine. Check out more details here.



Mother Bear's Pizza

Deep-dish pizzas & American snacks, plus beer & wine, at a local landmark resembling a rustic cabin..Visit their website here.



Anatolia

Cozy eatery with exotic decor preparing traditional Turkish specialties, plus a tea room. Get more details here.



Yogi's Grill & Bar

Guests can sample brews at this large pub with American eats & 50+ craft beers on tap. Check out further details here.



Anyetsang's Little Tibet Restaurant

This cozy eatery features Tibetan, Thai & Indian cuisine, plus tables on a garden patio. Find out more here.



The Owlery Restaurant

This is a vegetarian restaurant. Chill, arty choice featuring original vegetarian fare & craft beer, plus creative vegan baked goods. Get more details here.



Lennie's

Easygoing tavern serving local craft beers & a wide-ranging menu of eats, including gourmet pizzas. It is located right in the campus. Get more details here.



The Amrit India

This is one of the finest Indian restaurants in Bloomington. Serves tast tandoor and other indian specialities. Get more details here.



Fortune Cookies

Fortune Cookies is a famous chinese restaurant located right on campus. It has a wide variety at affordable rates. Get more details here.
