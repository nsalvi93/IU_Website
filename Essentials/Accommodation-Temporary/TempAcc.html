Accommodations

Temporary Accommodation Options in Bloomington

Indiana Memorial Union Biddle Hotel & Conference Center

The Indiana Memorial Union Biddle Hotel is at the famous IMU. IMU is located perfectly at the heart of the campus and thus it makes this location perfect for new students to get familiar with the campus. Check out more details here.


Quality Inn

This is a basic hotel for those who want affordable temporary accommodations. It has an indoor pool. Free continental service is also provided. Although far from campus, its a good bet for someone not wanting to spend much. Check out more details here.


Hilton Garden Inn Bloomington

Hilton Garden Inn is one of the best 3 star hotels out there. It is located in downtown and is 10-mintues walk away from the campus. Provides all modern rooms and services. Visit their website here.


Walnut Street Inn

Walnut Street Inn is small guesthouse with modern rooms and basic amenities. It is located in downtown. Visit their website here.



Comfort Inn

Comfort Inn is another basic hotel with an outdoor pool. It has modern rooms with free wifi. A free breakfast is also provided. Check out further details here.



Showers Inn

Showers Inn is a beautiful hotel located not very far from the university campus. It also provides free wifi and breakfast. Find out more here.



Super 8 Bloomington

Super 8 is a basic hotel located a little far from campus. But it makes up for this with its affordable room rates plus you get free wifi and continental breakfast. Get more details here.


Holiday Inn Bloomington

Holiday Inn Bloomington is a luxurious 3 star hotel. Its located a bit far from campus but has excellent amenities and modern rooms. Get more details here.



SpringHill Suites Bloomington

Springhill Suites, as the name suggests, is an all-suites hotel. Its not very far from campus and has all the modern room services. Find out more here here.



Americas Best Value Inn

Americas Best Value Inn has basic lodging and rooms. There is free wifi and continental breakfast. It is one of the most affordable options for temporary accommodations in Bloomington. Find out more here.
