Accommodations

Permanent Accommodation Options in Bloomington

On-campus Housing (IU-RPS)

The on-campus housing at IU Bloomington is under the Indiana University Residential Programs and Services. They have a range of options including residence halls and private apartments. Check out more details here.


Hoosier Court Apartments

Hoosier Court Apartments are one of the famous off-campus housing options for many students at IU Bloomington. It has a beautiful location and community. It has a wide variety of apartments ranging from 1 to 3 bedroom apartments. Although far from campus, it is a good option for students with their own vehicles. Check out more details here.

Fountain Park Apartments

Fountain park apartments are famous among many international students. It has a good location for proximity from campus and grocery stores. Bus connectivity is very good here. But apartment options are limited to 1 or 2 bed apartments.Visit their website here.

Park Doral Apartments

Park Doral Apartments are very famous for their location and proximity from campus as well as College Mall which is the prime shopping hub in Bloomington. They have many apartment options with affordable rents. But they don't provide in-house laundry options. Get more details here.

Hunter Ridge Apartments

Hunter Ridge apartments are located near to College Mall. They have many options in apartment styes ranging from 1 to 3 bedroom apartments. Check out further details here.



Woodbridge Apartments

Woodbridge apartments have good bus connectivity. It has all apartment types at affordable rents. Woodbridge is also pet-friendly neighborhood. Find out more here.

Reserve on Third

Reserve on third is an apartment community mostly for students. It has its own shuttle service which is a very good service. It offers many modern facilities but the rent is on the higher side. Get more details here.
