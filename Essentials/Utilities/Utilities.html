Utilities

Essential utility setup for apartments



Electricity

Electricity in Bloomington is provided by Duke Energy. When you move into your new apartment, you will need to set your electricity up. For this, you will need to contact Duke Energy to make sure the electricity is in your name the entire length of the lease.You can contact Duke at (800) 521-2232 or online here. If you do not have a social security number you may need to print out the ID Verification form on their website and send this to Duke Energy to set up the service in your name.


Water/Sewer

To start the water service at your apartment, you will need to fill out an application and fax it to City of Bloomington-Utilities to make sure that the water is in your name for the entire length of your lease. You can find the water application online here. To confirm receipt of this fax or to get your account number, please call (812) 349-3930.


Cable Internet and Phone

In Bloomington you will mostly have two options for these services. AT&T U-Verse or Comcast Communications. Please check with your rental agency or landlord which of these providers is available for your apartment. Visit here for Comcast details and here for AT&T U-Verse.
