Parks and Recreation

There are many Parks & recreational spots in Bloomington. Here are some of them:


Bryan Park

1001 S. Henderson St. Bloomington, IN 47401
Phone: 812-349-3705,Fax: 812-349-3705.Visit their website: here.



Cedar Bluff Nature Preserve

Just off Ketcham Road.Owned by The Nature Conservancy,Bloomington, IN 47403 Visit their website here.



Student Recreational Sports Center

This state-of-the-art, 204,000 square foot multi-purpose sports and fitness center features five basketball/volleyball courts, six outdoor tennis courts, three multi-purpose gyms and nine racquetball/squash courts, including two championship glass courts . SRSC hours are Mon. -Thurs. 6 am - 11:30 pm, Fri. 6 am - 9 pm, Sat. 8 am - 9 pm, Sun. 8 am - 11:30
Address: 1601 Law Lane,Bloomington IN 47405 Visit their website here.


Fairfax State Recreation Area

The recreation area features a swimming beach, boat ramp, and picnic areas.It is located on the west side of Monroe Lake.On Fairfax Road, accessible from Hwy 37 or South Walnut Street,Bloomington, IN 47401.Phone: 812-837-9546. Visit their website here.


Griffy Lake Nature Preserve

A beautiful and unique natural area, Griffy Lake is a 1,200-acre nature preserve located just minutes from downtown Bloomington. Its famous for hiking,fishing, boating and various others activitiess. Address: Griffy Lake Nature Preserve 3500 N. Hinkle Rd. Suite 250 Bloomington IN 47402 Phone: 812-349-3700 Fax: 812-349-3705 Visit their website here.


Hoosier National Forest

This 200,000-acre National Forest involves nine counties in South-Central Indiana. There are over 230 miles of hiking, horseback and mountain biking trails in the Forest. Hunting, nature study, camping picnicking and scenic drives are also famous activities.Visit their website here.

IU Outdoor Pool

The IU Outdoor Pool is open to the public Memorial Day through Labor Day. Address: 1833 N. Fee Ln. Bloomington, IN 47405 Phone: 812-855-9584 Check out their website here.



IU Tennis Center

The IU Tennis Center offers eight indoor tennis courts, lessons and clinics for all ages and skill levels, fitness equipment, racquet stringing, a pro shop and free parking is located on-site.Address:1833 North Fee Lane Bloomington IN 47408 Phone: 812-855-5750 Check out their website here.


Jackson Creek Park

Address: 5970 South Rogers St. Bloomington IN 47404 Phone: 812-349-2800 Check out their website here.




Leonard Springs Nature Park

Leonard Springs Nature Park is located on 95.5 acres on Leonard Springs Road. Highlights include Leonard and Shirley Springs, caves, steep forested slopes and a wetlands area.Check out their website here.
