Entertainment

There are many places for having fun in Bloomington.

The Comedy Attic

The Comdey Attic is Bloomington's first and only full time comedy club.It is also one of the most respected comedy club in the country and is usually in top 10 lists according to USA today 2014. Address:123 S. Walnut Street Bloomington IN 47408.Its the one mentioned as Bishop Bar in maps. Visit their website here.


Buskirk-Chumley Theater

Listed on the National Register of Historic Places, this 88-year-old Spanish mission revival silent movie house is one of the busiest community theaters in the country
Address:114 E. Kirkwood Avenue Bloomington IN 47408. Visit their website here.


Cardinal Stage Company

Cardinal Stage Company is focussed on improving professional theatre in Bloomington,Indiana. Address:900 S Walnut St, Bloomington, IN 47401. Visit their website here.


Bluebird Nightclub

A historic music venue booking high caliber talent in Bloomington
Address:The Bluebird Nightclub 216 North Walnut Street Bloomington, Indiana. Visit their website here.


Kilroy's Sports Bar

Bloomington's favorite bar, great food, great drinks, and daily specials.
Address:319 N. Walnut Street Bloomington IN 47404 Phone: 812-339-3006.Visit their website here.


Indiana Festival Theatre

The Indiana Festival Theatre is the summer professional theatre for the IU Department of Theatre and Drama. Performances take place at the Lee Norvelle Theatre and Drama Center on the campus of Indiana University Bloomington.


Indiana University Cinema

The Indiana University Cinema is a world-class venue. The cinemas has hosted many influential filmmakers like Werner Herzog, Albert Maysles, and Claire Denis. It has also hosted various famous actors and actresses like Meryl Streep ,Kevin Kline, and Glenn Close.. Check out their website here.
